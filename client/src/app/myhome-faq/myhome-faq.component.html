Basic Questions
1. When is our Trash Collection day in the week?

Garbage collection is handled by Republic Services and is collected every Friday weekly. All trash should be bagged and placed inside your gray refuse container which can be set out on the curb the night before your scheduled pickup and should be promptly put away once collection is complete.

2. What are utilities that I am responsible for?

As we have agreed, you are responsible for paying these utilities: Water, Gas, and Electricity. Additionally, they are splited among all residents in the house so please make sure not to waste any utilities if you are not using them.

3. What's the Neighborhood Like?

Our house is located in a walking-distance from the University of Toledo so please do not worry about your safety. Also, Toledo has some very nice parts about it. It has a good university and some nice public spaces like the art museum, the zoo, and the parks.
